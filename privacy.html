Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CharismeAI, we collect:

  • Screenshots and images you manually upload for analysis
  • Text you type into the app (messages, context notes)
  • A unique device identifier (used to track credits; no name or email required)
  • Usage data: credits used, features accessed, tone preferences
  • Device information: OS version, app version

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Contacts, location, or browsing history
  • Payment card details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered response generation from your screenshots
  • Store your reply history and favourite responses
  • Track credit balance and usage
  • Improve service quality and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services and Data Transmission

When you use AI features (Screenshot Analysis, Conversation Analysis, Generate Reply), your screenshots and typed text are transmitted in real time to OpenAI’s API for processing. This data leaves your device and is sent to OpenAI servers.

  • OpenAI (GPT-4.1-mini) — processes screenshots and text to generate reply suggestions. Per OpenAI’s API usage policy, data submitted via the API is not used to train their models
  • Firebase (Google) — handles anonymous authentication, stores your credit balance and usage history on Google Cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit

CharismeAI does not retain your screenshots after processing is complete. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

3a. Third-Party Data Protection

OpenAI and Firebase each operate under their own privacy policies and provide data protection standards equivalent to applicable privacy law requirements:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google / Firebase Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database access is protected by security rules
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing

5. Data Retention

  • Screenshots and responses: retained while your account is active
  • You can delete individual screenshots from your history at any time
  • Account deletion removes all associated data within 30 days

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Settings > Account)
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Request a data export by contacting tarikpanday20@gmail.com
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

7. Age Requirement

CharismeAI requires users to be 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18. If we learn that a user is under 18, we will promptly delete their account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
